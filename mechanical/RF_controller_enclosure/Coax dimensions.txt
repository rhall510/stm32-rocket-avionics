2.4GHz = 55mm coax length (start at 75mm - 10mm for connectors each end)
868MHz = 195mm coax length (start at 215mm - 10mm for connectors each end)

SMA connector assembly:
- Cut outer jacket 10mm from end
- Expose inner conductor 3mm
- Solder inner pin
- Add outer part and crimp on ferrule 
- Heat shrink
